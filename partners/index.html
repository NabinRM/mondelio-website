Naveego

Naveego® was founded to fill a market need. An IT services customer in the Oil & Gas industry needed a way to bring analytics-ready oil well production data from multiple facility sites into one place. They needed an analytics dashboard that they could trust to be accurate and error-free.

The solution was first implemented in the industry in 2012, and in 2014 a new company was spun off—Naveego®. Derek Smith, CTO and Co-Founder of Naveego®, has since architected next-generation technologies for the cloud-first data accuracy platform, creating a self-service data management solution driven by business rules (instead of technical limitations) for both non-technical and technical users.

The company that began by tackling CVS, MSSQL and NoSQL data sets can now connect to anything and everything. Naveego® entered the big data and IoT data markets in 2019 and has expanded from early success in the Oil & Gas industry into sixteen additional industry verticals.

Naveego® is poised to become the market leader in cleansing datasets used to train AI and machine learning applications.
