About Mondelio

Mondelio is an Australian company having commenced operations in 1982. Mondelio's single focus is to provide predictive data modelling and data analytics services to organisations throughout.

As the world turns and technology shifts in different directions, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to be relevant, progressive and in touch with what customers want and need.
