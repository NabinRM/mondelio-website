Our Services


Data Modeling

Mondelio uses standard data modelling techniques and methodologies to model data in a standard, consistent, predictable manner to enable it to be used in an easy and meaningful way. This is useful when organisations have large amounts of data to analyse and/or the data for analysis originates from multiple disparate sources. This gives customers the ability to easily mash up multiple data sources where each value in one field has a corresponding value in another field. This is true whether the data comes from one source, multiple sources or even from multiple physical locations.

Data Analytics

Mondelio consultants analyse the cleaned raw data to enable conclusions about the information. We assist in revealing trends & metrics that can be lost in the mass of information processed to improve overall efficiency of a business or their systems.

Data Cleansing

Our responsibility is to execute formal test plans to ensure the delivery of data related projects. Other responsibilities also include execution of data scripts and detailed analysis on these execution scripts. We track quality assurance metrics such as defects, defect counts, test results and test status.

Data Migration

Most data and digital transformation projects involve a process of selecting, preparing, extracting, and transforming data and (permanently or temporarily) transferring it from one computer storage system to another. Additionally, the validation of migrated data for completeness and accuracy are considered part of the entire data migration process.

Business Intelligence

As business intelligence analysts we use data to figure out market and business trends for companies to increase profits and efficiency. We look at large chunks of data and understand trends, and use tools to communicate these trends.

Data Visualisation

Our consultants take data models and convert these into visualisation widgets giving a graphic representation of tends in an organisation. It involves producing a variety of different images that communicate relationships among the represented data to organisational users of the data. This communication is achieved by mapping between available graphic tools and data values.

Project Management & Consulting

In addressing the key business issues, our consulting team will thoroughly investigate the challenges that present themselves and deliver a solution that meet customer expectations. The software is only part of the solution. Using our skills and experience combined with the software solutions we are able to provide exceptional professional services in the areas of corporate performance management, data analytics, data integration and data accuracy solutions.

Training

Our training services will allow you to:

  • Develop an understanding of the full feature set of Mondelio corporate performance suite and how its components inter-relate to support your business process
  • Learn how best to utilise our data accuracy & integration platform - Naveego to ensure the data accuracy of master records across the entire business
  • Learn how to use our data analytics solution - Sisense to integrate data from various sources and deliver business insights to all key stakeholders
Support

The Mondelio support team is ready to help with any enquiries pertaining to the Mondelio corporate performance suite, our data accuracy platform and our data analytics solution. We pride ourselves on being customer focused and know that timely and effective responses to support queries is what sets us apart and maintains the strong partnerships that we hold with all our clients.

Mondelio support has the ability to receive and log support requests via the web. A confirmation email will be generated to confirm we are working on your support request.
